How it works

We turn your past experience into a personalized practice session in just four steps.

1

We read your resume

Our system scans your uploaded file to quickly understand your skills, tools, and past experience.

2

We suggest roles

Based on your background, we recommend job titles that fit you best so you're practicing for the right positions.

3

You start practicing

You’ll answer technical questions based on your actual skills. If you’re doing well, the questions adjust and get harder—just like in a real interview.

4

We give you feedback

Once you finish, you get clear notes on what you answered well and exactly where you can improve before the real thing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the system handle my resume data?

Your privacy is our priority. We only process your resume to generate personalized interview sessions. We don't store your personal documents once the analysis is complete.

What kind of feedback do I receive?

You receive a detailed breakdown of your performance, including an overall success score, technical strength analysis, and specific actionable advice for improvement.

Does it support non-technical roles?

Currently, we are optimized for technical and engineering roles. We plan to expand our adaptive interview models to support other professional fields in the near future.
